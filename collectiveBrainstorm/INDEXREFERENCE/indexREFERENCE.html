I walk through the streets
of this new city

what I'm looking for
what I find
what finds me

While walking someone ask me:
Which communities do you feel part of?



I could not stop thinking
about that question.

And the more simple arise

what is a community?

Somehow the concept of community is not complete to me.
Somehow, even I see them all over around, I feel them weak.
Even the stronger ones.
And it's not personal. People lives and find their communities.
By decision, or by default.
It's social.
The concept of community has a cleft.
A rift.
Communities means all they mean. But also their bounderies.
